About Me

Hi, I'm Amanda Berry, a web designer and developer with a strong background in technology and problem-solving. I hold an Associate Degree in Electronics and Computers from DeVry University, along with a second degree in Information Technology and Networking, specializing in Automation and Electronic Systems, Information Systems and Programming, or Network Systems Administration. Additionally, I have an undergraduate certificate in Network Essentials.Originally from the westside of Chicago, I now reside in South Bend, IN. My technical skills are deeply rooted in my experience with SQL warehouse management systems and coding as a logistics technician. I’ve managed warehouse operations, including supply and demand logistics, shipping, receiving, inventory control, and customer service. Through these roles, I’ve developed a keen ability to navigate data systems, optimize processes, and contribute to efficient operations.

Beyond my technical expertise, I’m passionate about the impact of technology on security, education, healthcare, and business. I believe technology is a driving force for positive change, improving our daily lives and strengthening communities—especially those facing disadvantages. My goal is to use my skills to help build a more connected, informed, and thriving society.

My Services

UI / UX Design

Transforming ideas into engaging and intuitive experiences, my UI/UX design services focus on creating user-centered interfaces that delight and captivate. Through thorough research and user testing, I ensure that every design decision is backed by data, enhancing usability and fostering positive interactions. From wireframing to high-fidelity prototypes, I am dedicated to delivering designs that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also functionally robust.

Frontend Development

Bringing designs to life, my frontend development services specialize in crafting responsive, dynamic web applications that provide seamless user experiences across all devices. Utilizing modern frameworks and technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I ensure that every page is not only visually stunning but also optimized for performance and accessibility. My goal is to bridge the gap between design and functionality, creating a cohesive experience from start to finish.

Backend Development

On the server side, my backend development services ensure that your applications run smoothly and efficiently. With expertise in various programming languages and frameworks, I build robust APIs, manage databases, and implement server-side logic that powers your applications. Prioritizing security, scalability, and performance, I work diligently to create a solid foundation that supports your frontend and enhances overall user experience.

Consulting & Strategy

In addition to design and development, I provide consulting and strategic services to help businesses identify their unique needs and goals. Whether you’re looking to revamp an existing product or launch something new, I leverage my expertise to offer insights and recommendations tailored to your objectives. By collaborating closely with you, I ensure that the final product aligns with your vision and resonates with your target audience, setting the stage for success in a competitive landscape. Feel free to adjust any language to better fit your style or the specific nuances of your services!
